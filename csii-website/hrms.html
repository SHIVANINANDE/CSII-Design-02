Key Features & Functionalities

HR Database Management Maintain a centralized and secure repository of all employee records including personal details, qualifications, job history, documents, and more—accessible anytime, anywhere.
Recruitment Management Digitize end-to-end hiring workflows—requisition, job postings, application tracking, interview scheduling, and final selection—integrated with internal approvals and notifications.
Employee Onboarding Streamline onboarding with automated checklists, document submission, e-joining forms, orientation tracking, and provisioning—all in a paperless environment.
Performance Management Manage goal setting, KPIs, appraisals, 360° feedback, and performance reviews with configurable templates and performance analytics for better decision-making.
Benefits Administration Define and manage employee benefit plans including health insurance, provident fund, pension, gratuity, reimbursements, and more—aligned with statutory rules.
Workforce Management Track headcount, transfers, promotions, departmental mapping, organizational hierarchies, and workforce distribution—updated in real-time.
Time & Attendance Manage leave policies, accruals, requests, approvals, and balances through automated workflows with calendar integrations and escalation mechanisms.
Learning & Development Enable training programs, skill tracking, course enrolments, certification workflows, and feedback systems to foster continuous employee growth.
Talent Management Identify, retain, and nurture top talent through smart evaluations, career planning tools, and personalized development paths.
Succession Planning Create succession pipelines with visibility into key roles, employee readiness, risk profiles, and competency gaps—ensuring business continuity.
HR Analytics & Reporting Make data-driven decisions using advanced dashboards and reports on attrition, hiring trends, productivity, compliance, and workforce engagement.
Payroll Management Automate payroll with salary structures, tax compliance, deductions, reimbursements, payslip generation, and bank disbursements—error-free and audit-ready.
Employee Wellness Enable wellness programs, surveys, health records, and support systems to create a positive and healthy work culture.
Multi-Territory Workforce Management Manage employees across different geographies with location-specific policies, statutory compliance, language preferences, and localized workflows.
Employee Self-Service (ESS) Portal Empower employees with a mobile-friendly portal to update personal info, apply for leave, download payslips, raise tickets, view schedules, and more.
