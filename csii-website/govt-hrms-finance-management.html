Key Features of HRMS

Employee Master Database Centralized repository of employee profiles including service history, qualifications, designations, and more.
Leave & Attendance Management Real-time tracking and approval workflows for leave, holidays, and attendance records.
Service Book Automation Digital maintenance of employee service records with updates for promotions, transfers, and disciplinary actions.
Performance Appraisal System Role-based evaluation workflows and annual confidential report (ACR) tracking.
Recruitment & Onboarding Tools for managing vacancy posting, applications, selection processes, and digital joining formalities.
Transfer & Posting Management Facilitates automated transfer workflows based on policy, tenure, or vacancy mapping.
Pension & Retirement Processing Automated superannuation tracking, pension calculation, and gratuity processing.
Self-Service Employee Portal Employees can access pay slips, leave balances, apply for claims, and update personal details.

Key Features of Finance & Payroll Management

Automated Payroll Processing Accurate and timely salary generation with support for allowances, deductions, and tax computations.
Budgeting & Expenditure Monitoring Real-time tracking of fund allocations, expenditures, and department-wise financial performance.
GPF/CPF/NPS Tracking Contributions, interest calculations, withdrawals, and loan management.
Loan & Advance Management Application, approval, and EMI deduction management for staff loans.
Audit Trail & Compliance Reports System-generated reports for audit, taxation, and financial regulation compliance.
Integration with Treasury & Banks Seamless disbursal of payments through treasury systems or linked banks.
Voucher & Bill Generation Automated preparation and processing of bills, vouchers, and sanction orders.
