Understanding Water Tax Management

Water tax is a critical revenue stream for municipalities to maintain and expand water supply infrastructure. However, challenges like outdated records, manual billing, and poor collection systems can undermine service efficiency.

CSII India's Water Tax Management System offers a fully digital, integrated, and citizen-centric platform that boosts compliance, enhances user experience, and supports water resource sustainability.
