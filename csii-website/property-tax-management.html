Key Features & Functionalities

Online Assessment & Payment Enables property owners to assess and pay tax through a web portal or mobile app.
Automated Property Valuation Calculates tax based on property size, location, usage type, and applicable rates.
GIS Integration Geographic Information System (GIS) mapping for accurate property identification and tracking.
Digital Property Records Maintains a secure, centralized digital database of all properties.
Self-Assessment Tool Allows owners to estimate their property tax independently and transparently.
Unique Property Identification Number (UPIN) Each property is tagged with a unique ID for better traceability and updates.
Automated Notifications Sends SMS/email reminders for dues, receipts, and deadlines.
Reports & Analytics Generates customized reports for revenue forecasting, defaulter lists, and policy planning.

Understanding Property Tax Management

Property tax is a critical source of revenue for local governments, used to fund infrastructure, sanitation, roads, and essential public services. Traditional systems often face challenges such as outdated records, poor compliance, and manual inefficiencies.

CSII India's Property Tax Management System offers a modern, digital-first approach that helps cities unlock revenue potential, ensure fair taxation, and provide citizen-friendly tax services.
