Enforcing Urban Compliance. Empowering Smart Cities.

CSII India’s Unauthorized Construction Monitoring System is a digital governance solution designed to help municipal authorities identify, track, and act against illegal or non-compliant constructions. By enabling proactive surveillance, automated complaint handling, and integrated enforcement workflows, this system supports urban planning, ensures regulatory compliance, and protects public resources.

This module is part of CSII’s comprehensive e-Governance suite and is tailored to promote transparent, efficient, and citizen-friendly monitoring of urban development activities.

Understanding Unauthorized Construction

Unauthorized construction refers to any building activity conducted without proper permissions, in violation of zoning laws, or outside approved building plans. These activities can lead to safety risks, infrastructure strain, and legal complications.

With CSII India’s ERP-integrated system, municipalities can move away from fragmented, paper-based enforcement and instead adopt a smart, data-backed monitoring approach—ensuring timely action and sustainable urban growth.
