Use of "cookies"

Like other websites, CSII uses cookies. A cookie is a small piece of information sent to your computer by an organization and stored on your hard drive so that websites can recognize you when you visit them. They collect statistical data about your browsing behavior and habits.

Although cookies do not identify you as an individual, in some cases they may identify you as an individual when connected to third-party systems, such as marketing automation tools. Using such tools allows us to analyze individual’s behavioral patterns, present dynamic web content, and send them relevant and personalized emails.

Cookies can be disabled by configuring your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may result in loss of functionality when using our website and other websites.

Further, if you link to our website from a third-party website, we are not responsible for the privacy policies and practices of the owner and operator of such third-party website and recommend that you consult the policy of that third party website.
